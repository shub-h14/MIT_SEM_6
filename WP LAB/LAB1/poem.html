Whispers of the Night

The stars above are shining bright,
They guide the moon with gentle light.
The breeze it sings a lullaby sweet,
As dreams and silence softly meet.

The flowers close their sleepy eyes,
Beneath the calm and velvet skies.
Night’s whispers float on wings of air,
With tales of magic everywhere.

The world now sleeps in quiet peace,
And from the rush, it finds release.
The heart beats slow, the mind takes flight,
In dreams beneath the soft moon’s light.
